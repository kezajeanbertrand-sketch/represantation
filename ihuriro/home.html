IHURIRO
Murakaza neza kuri Ihuriro Public Secretary Ltd, urubuga rugamije gutanga serivisi zinoze kandi zizewe mu bijyanye na papeterie, imirimo yo mu biro, n’ubufasha bw’abanyamabanga ku bantu ku giti cyabo, ibigo byigenga, ndetse n’inzego za leta. Intego yacu ni ugufasha abakiriya kubona ibikoresho by’ibanze byo mu biro n’ubufasha bwa tekiniki mu buryo bwihuse, bwizewe kandi ku giciro cyiza.
Ku rubuga rwacu, abakiriya bashobora kubona amakuru arambuye ku bijyanye na serivisi dutanga. Muri zo harimo serivisi zo gucapa inyandiko zitandukanye, gutunganya inyandiko ku ikoranabuhanga rya mudasobwa, gukora kopi z’inyandiko, gusikana (scan) inyandiko, kohereza no kwakira emails, ndetse no gufasha abantu kuzuza inyandiko zisabwa mu nzego zitandukanye. Ibi byose bikorwa n’abakozi bacu bafite ubumenyi n’uburambe mu kazi k’ubunyamabanga n’ikorwa ry’inyandiko.
